About Me

Taurus Sun | Gemini Moon | Libra Rising

Hi, I'm Laura

A down-to-earth Taurus from ‘97, dance teacher, wellness-junkie & forever student of life.

I felt stuck in life for a very long time - ‘what should I do?’, ‘what is my purpose?’, ‘which way should I go?’. I could never really fit myself into one particular box. This constant search for myself led me to astrology.

My passion for astrology began at a very early age, but has been on the back burner for a long time (puberty, college, haha). As I entered my twenties, the flame started to burn again and I became a certified astrologer, attended a lot of webinars, devoured a lot of books and did a lot of birth chart readings.

Not only did I start to understand myself a lot more, but also the people around me. As I learned more about astrology, I began to see the world from a whole different point of view. I want to share that with other people and with you.
