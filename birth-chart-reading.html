Birth Chart Reading

This is a unique blueprint of the moment you were born. You can see it as a 'screenshot' of the sky and the exact positions of the planets at the time of your birth. In this reading, both your strengths and your challenges will emerge. It also shows how you can grow and what you need in this life. While interpreting this reading I look at the whole picture. I will pay attention to the personal planets: the sun, moon, Mercury, Venus and Mars. I also look at the social planets: Jupiter and Saturn. Finally, I also include the generation planets: Uranus, Neptune and Pluto. I will indicate these planets in house (life area) and in sign and how they are connected to each other. I also pay attention to the aspects, these are the relationships / dialogue that the planets have with each other and actually complete the personal story.

Who is this reading for?

This reading is for anyone who wants to take a deep dive into themselves. You do not need any prior knowledge of astrology for this reading. It is a great tool to use during your personal growth journey, because you actually get to know yourself and why you are the way you are, based on the stars.

Why astrology and this reading?

Astrology can give you new and refreshing insights that can empower you to move forward with the positive things in life. You can get so much out of a birth chart: what you need emotionally, what you are looking for in relationships, what your challenges are and where you get your happiness. It's a detailed, insightful and perzonalized in-depth reading all about you!

What will you receive?

You will receive a PDF file of approximately 20 pages. This will be emailed to the email address you provided. This way you can save it and read it back whenever you want. If you have a question you could always reach me by e-mail and ask a question afterwards.

What info do I need for this reading?

In order to read your birth chart, I need your date of birth, your exact time of birth and your place of birth.

*Please keep in mind that a time of birth that is exact to the minute is essential for the accurate interpretation of your birth chart. I highly recommend requesting a birth certificate from the municipality where you were born, since it states your exact time of birth.
