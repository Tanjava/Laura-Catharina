Career Reading

In this reading I will take a closer look at the parts of your birth chart that are related to career path. For example, I will look at the position of Mars, your Midheaven (MC), which is the most dominant planet in your birth chart, and your 10th house, as well as any other relevant planets.

Who is this reading for?

You do not need to have any prior knowledge of astrology for this reading. This reading is for anyone who desires direction in the field of career and work. With this reading I map out what topics (according to astrology) make you enthusiastic and passionate, what ultimately is what your social relevance is linked to, and where your talents and challenges lie.

Why this reading?

This reading gives different insights about yourself, so that you get to know yourself better. You can ultimately use this self-knowledge to shape your life in the way that is best for YOU. And in this case, it is specifically based on career themes.
