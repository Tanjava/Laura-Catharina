Big Three Reading

In this reading I look at the three most important personal 'planets' and points. I will incorporate the sun here, which represents your ego, identity and personality. I will also incorporate the moon, which represents your inner world and your emotions. Lastly, I will discuss your rising sign, also called your ‘ascendant’. This indicates how you behave towards the outside world. It is often said that this is the mask you wear. I see it more as the person you are when you walk into a room with people you don't know.

Who is this reading for?

You do not need to have any prior knowledge of astrology for this reading.This reading is suitable for anyone who wants to get acquainted with astrology, but does not want to dive deeper into it yet. It can tell you briefly about your character aptitude, about your strengths and weaknesses and about any challenges or talents.

Why this reading?

This reading is a nice stepping stone towards the journey of discovery to yourself. The more you learn about yourself, the more you know how to deal with your own energy. I myself have experienced that I can get a lot of strength from this self-knowledge, which I have been able to use in all facets of my life: work, relationships, free time, etc.
