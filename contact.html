Get in touch

If you have any questions or want to request an astrology reading, please feel free to send me a message and I'll get back to you asap!

Important: In order to get an accurate interpretation please let me know the following info when requesting a reading:

  • Birth Date (Please spell the month: e.g. 1 February 1980)
  • Birth Time (Exact)
  • Birth Place (City (+ State for US) and Country)
  • E.g. Ariana Grande, June 26th 1993 at 21:16 PM, Boca Raton (FL), United States

Please also let me know whether you want to receive your reading in English and/or Dutch. Your reading will be delivered to your email address within 30 days as a PDF file.

I look forward to getting your personalized astrology reading to you very soon!
